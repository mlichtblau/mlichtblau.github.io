In addition to my own projects I have made contributions to the following open source projects:

## [VM Portal](https://github.com/hpi-swt2/vm-portal)
In a group of 25 students we developed a vm management web application for the virtual machines hosted at Hasso Plattner Institute. We used Ruby on Rails.

## [Smile](https://github.com/bptlab/smile), [CAZ](https://github.com/bptlab/caz)
In a group of seven students we are developing a platform to optimise the last mile delivery of parcels. Smile and CAZ are Node.js servers, that help manage resources and communication with external interfaces

## [Chimera](https://github.com/bptlab/chimera), [Unicorn](https://github.com/bptlab/Unicorn)
Our bachelors project is based on the business process execution tool Chimera and the event processing platform Unicorn, which we modify and extend to our use case.

## [Squeak Polymorphic Identifiers](https://github.com/hpi-swa-lab/squeak-polymorphic-identifiers)
For a programming language lab I implemented the concept of Polymorphic Identifiers in Squeak. I extended the Squeak compiler to accept and use the new variable notation.
